Why need background noise removal online

Good background noise removal tools will boost audience engagement rate and conversion.

Video and audio recordings

Background noise is almost always present in recorded speeches, interviews, podcasts, and music recordings, especially if these files were recorded in the past when there wasn't advanced active noise cancellation software in recording devices.

Phone calls

Phone calls are subject to a lot of disturbance from ambient noises as well as radio wave frequencies adding to the noise. This makes recorded phone calls really difficult to comprehend and decipher, especially if they are tapped.

Voiceovers and narrations

When adding a voiceover or narration to a video, it is imperative that there is absolutely no distracting background noise so that listeners can have the visceral sensation and engaging experience while focusing on the video.
